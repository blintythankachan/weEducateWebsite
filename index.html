Scholorship and internship for needed students

We help to get scholarship and internship for the students who show academic merit in their respective fields.scholarship is a grant of money to study at a college or university. Scholarships are often awarded on the basis of academic merit or because the student is part of a group. For example, there are scholarships for the children of firefighters. An internship is a job. It is supposed to provide insight into a career so students can gain experience that leads to more job opportunities after graduation. Some internships are paid; Some are not. Internships are meant to be temporary - either summer work or part-time during the school year.
